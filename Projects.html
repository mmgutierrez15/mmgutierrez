Voting Site Network Analysis

For this project I was tasked to find four optimal
locations for voting sites. Optimality was defined by
reaching 70% of eligible voters in San Francisco with
reachability being determined by a site being 5 minutes
away from any site. The propsective sites were limited to
popular restuarants in San Francisco as they already would
have a baseline level of accessibilty.
